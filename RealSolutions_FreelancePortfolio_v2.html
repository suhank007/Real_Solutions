Common questions

FAQ

What's the minimum engagement size?
For day-rate work, the minimum is 5 days. For project-based scopes, work typically starts around €2,500. Discovery calls are always free — no minimum whatsoever.
Remote or on-site? How does that work?
Both. Most analytical and engineering work is done remotely. Governance-heavy engagements — C-suite steering, PLM migrations — may include on-site days at the standard €600/day rate. Based in Paris, available across Europe.
How quickly can you start?
Immediately. After discovery and scoping (48–72 hrs), work begins the same week. For longer programme embedding roles, start date is agreed as part of the SOW.
Do you sign NDAs before discovery calls?
Yes, always — before any substantive conversation. All client data is treated as strictly confidential. Dashboard demos shown use dummy data to demonstrate methodology without breaching NDA obligations.
What industries do you specialise in?
Aviation, Manufacturing & PLM, Insurance, Pharma, Luxury, and Clinical Research. Nine years of vertical-specific knowledge means zero learning curve — we already understand your data challenges.
Can I see a live dashboard demo before committing?
Absolutely — free 30-minute live demos showing real interactive platforms built for Air France, Atlas Copco, and Qatar Airways with dummy data. Use the contact form below to request one.
Do you offer fixed-price projects?
Yes. For well-defined scopes — a Power BI platform, a data architecture review, a PLM readiness assessment — we provide fixed-price quotes. Budget certainty before any commitment.
What's the retainer exactly, and can I cancel?
€800/month covers 2 structured sessions, async Slack access, and strategic advisory. Cancel with 30 days notice. No lock-in — designed for orgs that need executive-level guidance without a full-time hire.
